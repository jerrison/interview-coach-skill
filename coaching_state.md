#### S001: T-Mobile OEM Portal
**Company:** T-Mobile | **Role:** Senior PM, IoT Platform
**Situation:** IoT OEM co-selling channel worked for enterprise but couldn't serve SMBs. Noticed $15M+ in SMB deals sitting unactioned in Salesforce — ops deprioritized them because manual onboarding made each deal unprofitable.
**Task:** Unlock the SMB segment by removing the structural bottleneck that capped channel growth.
**Action:** Interviewed SMBs and mapped ops breakdowns. Built business case for three options; recommended self-serve portal. Drove end-to-end: OEM discovery, UX design, API specs, migration plan with Director of Ops.
**Result:** Unlocked $15M+ SMB pipeline. Sunsetted manual approach.
**Earned Secret:** The most valuable market opportunities often don't show up in product metrics — they show up in pipeline queues and in the frustrations of the people closest to the manual work.
**++:** Applied same lens at Moody's → discovered SlipStream opportunity by noticing underwriter bottleneck in sales notes.

**Earned Secret:** Better risk separation breaks the false binary between growth and profitability entirely.
